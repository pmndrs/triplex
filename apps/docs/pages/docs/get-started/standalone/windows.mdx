## Installation

1. Download [Triplex Standalone for Windows](/download)
1. Double-click the installer
1. After the installer completes Triplex will automatically open
1. Open Triplex again through the start menu
1. Pin Triplex to the taskbar by right-clicking the app and clicking Select more > Pin to taskbar

## Common Questions

### Why is there a trust warning after downloading?

The Windows distributable is currently unsigned, however when enough users have marked it as safe Windows will no longer consider it a threat and not show the warning.

Marking it as safe will let you continue installing it.
