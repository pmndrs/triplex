## Installation

1. Download [Triplex Standalone for macOS](/download)
1. Double-click the app
1. Drag `Triplex.app` into the Applications folder, making it available in the macOS launchpad
1. Open Triplex by double clicking the icon
1. Add Triplex to your Dock by right-clicking on the icon in the dock choosing Options > Keep in Dock.

## Common Questions

### Does Triplex run on Apple silicon machines?

Yes! Triplex runs on Apple silicon M1/M2 machines.
